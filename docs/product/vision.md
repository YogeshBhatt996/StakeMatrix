# Product Vision

## One-line pitch
> A centralised, multi-user stakeholder register that keeps every project team aligned — from day one to delivery.

## Problem statement
Project teams waste hours maintaining stakeholder information in disconnected spreadsheets. Contact details go stale, influence levels are guessed, FTE counts diverge between documents, and there is no single source of truth accessible to everyone on the team.

## Vision statement
StakeMatrix gives every project team a single, always-current register of their stakeholders — who they are, how to reach them, how much influence they hold, and how much FTE capacity they represent. Admins control access; team members stay informed.

## Target users

| Persona | Role | Primary need |
|---------|------|--------------|
| Project Manager | Creates/owns projects | Centralised view of all stakeholders and FTEs |
| Team Member | Contributes to a project | View and edit their assigned project's register |
| Admin | System administrator | Manage users, grant access, export data |

## Success metrics
- All active project stakeholders recorded in one place
- Signed vs Deployed FTE visible at a glance
- Zero password-sharing (each user has their own login)
- Monthly email reminder keeps data fresh
- Excel export accepted by stakeholders in < 1 click

## Out of scope (v1)
- Real-time collaboration / presence indicators
- Stakeholder communication logging
- JIRA / Teams / Outlook integration
- Mobile-native app
